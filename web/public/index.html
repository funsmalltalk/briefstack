FAQ

Good things to know

Do I need my own study materials?
No. BriefStack can search the internet for content on any topic. Upload your own PDFs, slides, or notes to make briefs even more personalized — or skip it entirely and use internet-only mode.
You are not the product.
BriefStack charges a subscription fee. We don't run ads, we don't sell your data, and we don't use your uploaded files to train AI models. Your materials and context are yours. We process them only to generate your brief, and nothing else. Compare that to "free" tools that monetize your attention and data — here you pay a fair price and the product serves you, not advertisers.
How do I know the content is accurate?
BriefStack uses Claude (Anthropic's AI) to write briefs grounded in real news and your source materials. Every brief includes a "Real World Hook" with a named company or event you can verify. That said, AI content can contain errors — treat each brief as a starting point for thinking, not a source of ground truth. We include an AI disclaimer in every email. If a brief has a factual issue, you can skip it and the next one won't repeat the topic.
What if I want to change my voice or topics?
Change anything in your dashboard, anytime. Switch from Galloway to Howard Marks to Kara Swisher mid-week. Your next brief uses the new settings immediately.
Can I control how often I receive briefs?
Yes. You can choose daily (7 days/week), weekdays only (Mon–Fri), Mon/Wed/Fri, or weekly (Mondays). Set your preferred send time and timezone in the dashboard.
Can I pause or cancel?
Yes, anytime. Toggle emails on/off instantly in your dashboard. Cancel your subscription in one click — no emails, no forms, no friction. Your data stays accessible until the end of your billing period.
Is my data safe?
Your uploaded files are processed to generate your brief and are not shared with anyone. We use Anthropic's API (Claude) and Google's API (Gemini) for generation — your topic context is sent to these services per their standard privacy terms. We store your email, settings, and brief history. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
