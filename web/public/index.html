Questions

Good things to know

Do I need my own study materials?
No. BriefStack searches the web for content on any topic you choose. Upload your own PDFs, slides, or notes to personalize it further - or skip that and go internet-only.
What if I want to change my voice or topics?
Change anything in your dashboard at any time. Switch from Galloway to Howard Marks to Kara Swisher mid-month. Your next email uses the new settings immediately.
Is my data safe?
Your uploaded files are yours. We process them to generate your briefs and don't share or sell your data to anyone.
Can I pause or cancel?
Yes, anytime. Toggle emails on/off in your dashboard. Cancel your subscription in one click - no emails, no forms, no friction.
