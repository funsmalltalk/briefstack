Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: Your uploaded files are yours. We use your data only to send your daily brief. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with third parties except what's needed to operate the service (AI processing, email delivery).

1. What We Collect

When you use BriefStack, we collect:

  • Email address — used to send your daily brief and authentication links
  • Your context text — the "about you" paragraph you provide to personalize your briefs
  • Uploaded files — PDFs or documents you upload as source material
  • Settings — your persona choice, send schedule, and topic preferences
  • Usage data — emails sent, dates, and subjects (stored for your history view)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Generate and send your personalized daily brief
  • Authenticate you via magic link emails
  • Display your email history in the dashboard
  • Improve the service (aggregate, non-identifiable usage patterns only)

3. Your Uploaded Files

Files you upload are used only to generate your personalized content. We do not:

  • Share your files with other users
  • Use your files to train AI models
  • Sell or license your files to any third party

You can delete your uploaded files at any time from your dashboard. You own your content.

4. Third-Party Services

To operate BriefStack, we share data with these services:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — your topic context and persona settings are sent to generate content. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini API) — used for header image generation (topic concept only, no personal data). Google Privacy Policy
  • Email delivery provider — your email address is used to deliver your brief
  • Railway (hosting) — our server infrastructure. Data is stored in the US.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or data brokers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as you have an account. Your last 30 email briefs are stored for your history view. You can delete your account and all associated data by emailing us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails (unsubscribe link in every email)

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@briefstack.app.

7. Cookies

We use a single session cookie (bs_token) to keep you logged in for 30 days. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

BriefStack is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at hello@briefstack.app.
